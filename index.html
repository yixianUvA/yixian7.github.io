Bio

I am currently navigating the final year of my Ph.D. studies at the Parallel Computing Systems Group, supervised by Prof. Andy. D. Pimentel and Dr. Anuj Pathania. Before embarking on my Ph.D., I completed my master's degree at the Sun Yat-sen University under the guidance of Prof. Yutong Lu and Prof. Yunfei Du. My academic journey began at the Communication University of Zhejing, where I was mentored by Dr. Jinlong Li during my bachelor's studies..
