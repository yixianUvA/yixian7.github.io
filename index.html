Research

I am broadly interested in computer security, privacy, and systems research. My current interests include the following domains:

  • Systems security: audit logging and forensics for Advanced Persistent Threat (APT) attacks (USENIX ATC'18, USENIX Security'18, VLDB'19 Demo)
  • Network security: attacks and defenses in online social networks (ACM CCS'15, MIS2'18, IEEE CNS'18)
  • Mobile security: intention-behavior discrepancy analysis of mobile apps (ICSE'19, ACM CCS'19)
  • Blockchain: blockchain interoperability (ACM CCS'19)
  • Privacy: GDPR and data privacy compliance (POLY'19)

I enjoy building scalable systems to solve real-world security and pricacy problems. My work has been adopted by companies like Microsoft, NEC Laboratories America, and Facebook.
